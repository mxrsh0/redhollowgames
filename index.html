The Journal

You are paranormal investigators operating from an RV, tracking supernatural activity across a forest filled with unknown entities.

Deploy cameras, sensors, and audio tools to trigger behaviours and capture evidence at the exact moment it occurs.

Each entity follows hidden rules. Some will mislead you, forcing players to experiment and verify what is real.

Return to the RV, analyse findings, and unlock journal entries that reveal strengths and weaknesses.

Planned for PC, with future console release in consideration.
