Who I am

I’m the developer behind Red Hollow Games, an upcoming indie studio focused on immersive gameplay and atmospheric worlds. I’m currently developing my first major release, Nightfall Protocol.

Nightfall Protocol

Nightfall Protocol is a zombie survival open-world simulator centered around exploration, scavenging, and long-term survival. The game emphasizes player choice, dynamic threats, and systems that reward preparation, adaptability, and risk management.

The goal is to create a sandbox where unpredictable situations and meaningful decisions allow players to create their own stories—whether that’s fortifying a safe zone, venturing into dangerous territory, or barely surviving a night that didn’t go according to plan.
