Bones & Bluff

Bones & Bluff is a multiplayer card game built around the core idea of Liar’s Table. Players must bluff, deceive, and read one another to survive each round, knowing that every move could be a lie—or the truth.

The game is designed around tension, mind games, and player interaction, where calling a bluff at the right moment can change everything. Matches are fast, competitive, and driven entirely by player decisions.

Bones & Bluff is currently in development and is planned for release on console platforms.
