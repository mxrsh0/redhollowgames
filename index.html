Who I am

Hi — I’m the creator behind Red Hollow Games. I’m an upcoming indie developer focused on building games that lean into atmosphere, tension, and player-driven stories. I’m currently working toward my first major release: Nightfall Protocol.

Nightfall Protocol

Nightfall Protocol is a zombie survival open-world simulator built around exploration, scavenging, and long-term survival strategy. The goal is to create a world that feels alive (even when it’s falling apart): dynamic threats, meaningful decision-making, and systems that encourage you to adapt—whether you’re securing supplies, planning routes, managing risk, or turning a fragile shelter into something sustainable.

The experience is meant to reward careful preparation while still leaving room for unpredictable moments: a quiet supply run that suddenly becomes a sprint, a safe area that stops being safe, or a small choice that changes what happens next. Over time, I want Nightfall Protocol to grow into a sandbox where players can build their own stories through the systems and the world itself.
